# SOP: Customer Ordering Guide

**Document ID:** SOP-MANUAL-CUSTOMER-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** March 13, 2026  
**Department:** Customer Experience  
**Applies To:** All Customers (Registered and Guest)

---

## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure provides step-by-step instructions for customers to browse the menu, place orders, manage tabs, track orders, use rewards, and manage their profile on the Wawa Garden Bar ordering platform.

---

## Scope

This SOP covers:
- Accessing the platform and signing in
- Browsing the menu and adding items to cart
- Placing Dine-in, Pickup, Delivery, and Pay Now orders
- Opening and managing bar tabs
- Tracking order status
- Using rewards and loyalty points
- Managing your profile and addresses
- Requesting data deletion

---

## Prerequisites

- A smartphone, tablet, or computer with a web browser
- Internet connection
- For registered accounts: a valid email address or phone number

---

## Procedure

### Part 1: Getting Started

#### 1.1 Access the Platform
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Wawa Garden Bar website
2. The **Home Page** (`/`) displays branding, logo, and order-type feature cards (Dine In, Pickup, Delivery)
3. Tap **"View Menu"** to start browsing

#### 1.2 Sign In or Continue as Guest

**Option A — Registered / New Account (Passwordless):**
1. Tap **"Sign In"** or navigate to `/login`
2. Enter your **email address** or **phone number**
3. A **4-digit PIN** will be sent to your email, SMS, or WhatsApp
4. Enter the PIN to verify your identity
5. You are now logged in — your session lasts 7 days

**Option B — Continue as Guest:**
1. Tap **"Continue as Guest"**
2. You can browse, order, and pay without creating an account
3. Your email is saved automatically on your first order — you can claim your guest orders later by registering with the same email

---

### Part 2: Browsing the Menu

#### 2.1 Navigate the Menu (`/menu`)
1. The menu is organised by **main categories**: Food and Drinks
2. **Food subcategories:** Starters, Main Courses, Rice Dishes, Swallow, Soups, Pepper Soup, Noodles, Sauce, Small Chops, Desserts, Sides
3. **Drinks subcategories:** Beer (Local/Imported/Craft), Wine, Soft Drinks, Water, Juice, Energy Drink, Malt, Yoghurt, Cocktails, and more
4. Use the **category navigation bar** to quickly jump between sections
5. Use the **search bar** to find items by name

#### 2.2 View Item Details
1. Tap on any menu item to see:
   - High-quality image
   - Description
   - Price (₦)
   - Dietary tags (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free)
   - Allergen warnings
   - Spice level
   - Preparation time
2. Some items offer **portion options** (Full, Half, Quarter) with different pricing

#### 2.3 Add Items to Cart
1. Select your desired **portion size** (if available)
2. Choose any **customisations** (size, add-ons, extras)
3. Add **special instructions** (e.g., "No onions", "Extra spicy")
4. Set the **quantity**
5. Tap **"Add to Cart"**
6. A cart icon in the top-right corner shows your item count

---

### Part 3: Reviewing Your Cart

1. Tap the **Cart icon** to open the cart sidebar
2. Review:
   - All items, quantities, and customisations
   - Portion sizes (Full/Half/Quarter badges)
   - Running subtotal
3. Adjust quantities using **+** / **−** buttons
4. Remove items by tapping the **remove** button
5. When satisfied, tap **"Checkout"**

---

### Part 4: Checkout

The checkout is a **multi-step flow**. The steps displayed depend on your order type and tab status.

#### Step 1: Customer Information
1. Enter your **name** (required)
2. Enter your **email** (optional but recommended for receipts)
3. Enter your **phone number** (optional)
4. If you are logged in, these fields may be **pre-filled** from your profile

#### Step 2: Order Type Selection
Choose one of four order types:

| Order Type | Icon | Description | Extra Info Required |
|------------|------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Dine In** | 🍽️ | Eat at the restaurant | Table number |
| **Pickup** | 📦 | Collect your order | Preferred pickup time |
| **Delivery** | 🚚 | Delivered to you | Delivery address |
| **Pay Now** | ⚡ | Quick order & pay | None |

#### Step 3: Order-Type-Specific Details

**Dine-in:**
1. Enter your **table number**
2. Proceed to **Tab Options** (see Part 5 below)

**Pickup:**
1. Select your **preferred pickup time**

**Delivery:**
1. Enter your **delivery address** (street, city, state, postal code)
2. Add **delivery instructions** (optional)
3. Delivery fee applies: Base ₦1,000, reduced ₦500, free above ₦2,000

**Pay Now:**
- No additional details required — proceeds directly to payment

#### Step 4: Special Instructions (All Order Types)
1. Add any dietary requirements or preparation preferences
2. Examples: "Nut allergy", "Gluten-free", "Less salt"

#### Step 5: Tip (Optional — not shown for tab orders)
1. Select a tip amount or enter a custom amount
2. Tips are added to your order total

#### Step 6: Payment Method (not shown for tab orders)
Choose from:
- **Pay with Card**
- **Pay with Transfer**
- **Pay with USSD**
- **Pay with Phone Number**

#### Step 7: Review and Confirm
1. Review your complete order summary:
   - Items, quantities, prices
   - Service fee (2%)
   - Tax (if applicable, 7.5% VAT)
   - Delivery fee (delivery orders only)
   - Tip
   - Total
2. Tap **"Place Order"** or **"Proceed to Payment"**

#### Step 8: Payment
1. Complete payment through the selected method (Monnify gateway)
2. On success, you will see an **Order Confirmation** page with your order number

### Part 5: Dine-In Tab Management

Tabs allow you to place multiple orders over a visit and pay once at the end.

#### 5.1 Opening a New Tab
1. During dine-in checkout, select **"Open a Tab"** on the Tab Options step
2. Your order is created and a new tab is opened for your table
3. You are redirected to the **tab details page** confirming the tab is open

#### 5.2 Adding Orders to an Existing Tab
1. If you already have an open tab, the system **automatically detects it**
2. The Tab Options step will show: **"Add to Existing Tab"** (pre-selected)
3. You **cannot** open a second tab or pay separately while a tab is open
4. Complete checkout — the order is added to your tab and the tab total is updated
5. Tip and payment steps are **skipped** (payment happens when you close the tab)

#### 5.3 Viewing Your Tab
1. Navigate to **Orders** → **Tabs** (`/orders/tabs`)
2. Your open tab(s) are listed with:
   - Tab number and table number
   - All orders on the tab
   - Running total
3. Tap a tab to see full details and individual orders

#### 5.4 Adding More Orders from the Tab Page
1. From the tab details page, tap **"Add Order to Tab"**
2. You are taken to the menu to select items
3. Complete checkout — the order is added to the existing tab

#### 5.5 Closing and Paying Your Tab
1. From the tab details page, tap **"Pay Tab"**
2. Enter your **name** and **email** (pre-filled if available)
3. **Select rewards** to apply (if you have any active rewards)
4. Review the tab summary:
   - All orders and items
   - Subtotal, service fee, tax
   - Reward discount (if applied)
   - Tip (optional)
   - Final total
5. Select a **payment method** and complete payment
6. Tab is closed and marked as **"Paid"**

#### 5.6 Important Tab Rules
- **One tab per customer:** You cannot have multiple open tabs simultaneously
- **Must add to tab:** If you have an open tab, new dine-in orders must go on that tab
- **Close first:** To pay separately for a new order, close your existing tab first
- **Ask staff:** Staff can also manage your tab from the dashboard

### Part 6: Tracking Your Order

#### 6.1 Order Status Page (`/order`)
After placing an order, you can track its real-time status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Order received, awaiting confirmation |
| **Confirmed** | Order confirmed by staff |
| **Preparing** | Kitchen is preparing your order |
| **Ready** | Order is ready for pickup / serving |
| **Out for Delivery** | (Delivery only) Driver is on the way |
| **Delivered** | (Delivery only) Order has been delivered |
| **Completed** | Order complete |

- Status updates are delivered in **real-time** — no need to refresh
- You may receive notifications via email or WhatsApp if configured

#### 6.2 Order History (`/orders`)
1. Navigate to **Orders** from the main navigation
2. View all your past and current orders
3. Tap any order to see full details:
   - Items ordered
   - Amounts and fees
   - Payment status
   - Status timeline

### Part 7: Rewards and Loyalty

#### 7.1 How You Earn Rewards
- Rewards are triggered **automatically** when your order meets configured spend thresholds
- Each qualifying order has a **probability** of generating a reward (set by the business)
- Rewards are issued after successful payment

#### 7.2 Types of Rewards
| Type | What You Get |
|------|-------------|
| **Percentage Discount** | e.g., 10% off your next order |
| **Fixed Discount** | e.g., ₦500 off your next order |
| **Free Item** | A complimentary menu item |
| **Loyalty Points** | Points added to your balance |

#### 7.3 Viewing Your Rewards (`/profile/rewards`)
1. Navigate to **Profile** → **Rewards**
2. **Active Rewards** tab shows:
   - Reward code
   - Reward type and value
   - Expiry date (with warnings for soon-expiring rewards)
   - "Use Now" / "Apply at Checkout" buttons
3. **Reward History** tab shows all past rewards with status (Active, Redeemed, Expired)

#### 7.4 Using a Reward at Checkout
1. During the checkout **Review** step, your applicable rewards are displayed
2. Select the reward(s) you want to apply
3. The discount is calculated and shown in the order summary
4. Complete payment for the reduced total

#### 7.5 Using a Reward on a Tab
1. When paying your tab, the **Reward Selector** is shown
2. Select one or more active rewards
3. The reward discount is deducted from the tab total before payment

#### 7.6 Loyalty Points
- **Earning:** Points are awarded on qualifying orders
- **Balance:** Visible on your rewards page — shows total points, earned, and spent
- **Redemption:** 100 points = ₦1 — redeemable at checkout
- **Points per item:** Some menu items have specific point values

### Part 9: Privacy and Data Management

#### 9.1 Privacy Policy (`/privacy`)
- Review the Wawa Garden Bar privacy policy at any time

#### 9.2 Data Deletion Request (`/data-deletion`)
1. You must be **logged in** to request data deletion
2. Navigate to the data deletion page
3. Your email/phone is pre-filled and locked (cannot be changed)
4. Submit your deletion request
5. **Important:** You cannot submit a deletion request if you have:
   - An **open tab** — close and pay it first
   - **Active orders** (pending, confirmed, preparing, ready, or out-for-delivery) — wait for completion
6. An admin will process your request and you will receive a confirmation email

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Quick Drink at the Bar
1. Add drink to cart → Checkout → Select **"Pay Now"** → Enter name → Pay → Done

### Scenario 2: Dine-In Dinner with Friends (Tab)
1. Add items to cart → Checkout → Select **"Dine In"** → Enter table number → **"Open a Tab"** → Order placed
2. Later: add more items → Checkout → **"Add to Existing Tab"** (auto-selected) → Order placed
3. Ready to leave: go to **Orders → Tabs** → Tap tab → **"Pay Tab"** → Apply rewards → Pay → Tab closed

### Scenario 3: Delivery Order
1. Add items to cart → Checkout → Select **"Delivery"** → Enter address → Add instructions → Tip → Pay → Track delivery in real-time

### Scenario 4: Pickup Order
1. Add items to cart → Checkout → Select **"Pickup"** → Choose pickup time → Enter name → Pay → Collect when status shows "Ready"

### Scenario 5: Using a Reward
1. Place an order that meets the spend threshold → Reward issued automatically
2. Next visit: at checkout, select the reward → Discount applied → Pay reduced total

### Scenario 6: Guest Ordering
1. Tap "Continue as Guest" → Browse menu → Add items → Checkout → Enter name and email → Pay
2. Later: Register with the same email → Your guest orders are linked to your account

## Troubleshooting

### I can't open a new tab
**Cause:** You already have an open tab on another table.  
**Solution:** Close your existing tab first (pay it), then open a new one.

### My order is stuck on "Pending"
**Cause:** The kitchen hasn't confirmed it yet.  
**Solution:** Wait a few minutes. If it persists beyond 10 minutes, ask staff for assistance.

### I can't find my order
**Solution:** Navigate to **Orders** from the navigation bar. If you ordered as a guest, make sure you're using the same browser session.

### Payment failed
**Solution:** Try a different payment method (Card, Transfer, USSD). Ensure sufficient funds. If the issue persists, ask staff to process a manual payment.

### Reward not appearing at checkout
**Cause:** The reward may have expired, or the order doesn't meet the reward's conditions.  
**Solution:** Check your reward's expiry date on the Rewards page (`/profile/rewards`). Some rewards have minimum spend requirements.

### I want to cancel my order
**Solution:** Contact staff directly. Only staff can cancel orders from the dashboard. Orders that have already been paid cannot be cancelled (a refund process applies).

### Delivery address not accepted
**Cause:** The address may be outside the delivery radius.  
**Solution:** Try a different address within the delivery area, or switch to Pickup.
